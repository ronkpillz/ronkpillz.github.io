Omnistep Footwear Sales Performance Dashboard

This project involved developing an interactive Power BI dashboard for Omnistep Footwear, a retail footwear company seeking to improve sales reporting and business decision-making. The solution transformed raw sales data into actionable insights using Power Query, Star Schema data modelling, and DAX measures. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators, evaluate product performance, understand customer behaviour, and identify revenue opportunities through interactive visualizations.

Business Problem

As Omnistep Footwear expanded its operations across multiple countries, products, and customer segments, management needed a faster and more effective way to monitor business performance. Manual analysis of large sales datasets made it difficult to identify trends, evaluate product performance, understand customer purchasing behaviour, and make timely strategic decisions. The objective of this project was to develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that transforms sales data into meaningful business insights for informed decision-making.

Key Insights

  • Strong Overall Sales Performance: Omnistep Footwear generated over $9 million in Net Revenue, processed approximately 30,000 customer orders, and sold more than 75,000 units, demonstrating strong market demand and healthy business performance.
  • Lifestyle Products Drive Revenue: Lifestyle products generated the highest revenue among all product categories, indicating that customers have a stronger preference for casual and everyday footwear.
  • Retail Stores Slightly Outperform Online Sales: While both channels performed well, Retail Store sales slightly exceeded Online sales, suggesting opportunities to further strengthen the online sales channel.
  • Bank Transfer is the Preferred Payment Method: Customers showed a strong preference for Bank Transfer, highlighting the importance of maintaining reliable digital payment services.
  • Balanced Customer Income Distribution: Revenue was relatively balanced across Low-, Medium-, and High-income customer groups, indicating that the company successfully serves a diverse customer base.
  • High-Demand Shoe Sizes: Shoe sizes 7, 11, 9, 6, and 10 generated the highest revenue, providing valuable insights for inventory planning and stock management.

Business Recommendations

  • Increase marketing investment and product availability for the Lifestyle product category to maximize future revenue growth.
  • Prioritize inventory for high-demand shoe sizes (7, 11, 9, 6, and 10) to reduce stock shortages and improve customer satisfaction.
  • Strengthen the Online sales channel through targeted digital marketing campaigns, promotional offers, and an enhanced online shopping experience.
  • Continue improving digital payment infrastructure while maintaining multiple secure payment options for customers.
  • Maintain marketing strategies that appeal to customers across all income levels to preserve a balanced customer base.
  • Use the interactive Power BI dashboard as a continuous decision-support tool for monitoring business performance and identifying emerging sales trends.

Business Impact

The Power BI solution transformed raw sales data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, evaluate product performance, understand customer behaviour, and make informed business decisions. The dashboard provides a centralized reporting solution that supports strategic planning, inventory optimization, and continuous business performance monitoring.
